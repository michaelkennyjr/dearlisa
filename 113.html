113 days to go

Happy Anniversary, Lisa!

It's hard to believe we've been together for five years. I look back at old pictures of us from way back in 2014, and I realize that I've spent half a decade spinning in this whirlwind. In a flash, I went from wondering when I would find love to being in a relationship with the woman that I'd eventually spend the rest of my life with.

It's pretty crazy when you think about it. I know online dating wasn't quite as easy for you, but I think back to the person I was on New Year's Day 2014 and I wonder what I would have said if you had told me that I was less than a month from meeting my wife. It just goes to show that our lives can change instantly upon the smallest decisions, even if we don't realize it at the time.
